raytracing fps
This FPS game utilizes principles of raytracing for its raygun blast mechanics. In the game your raygun has three different modes: diffuse, glass and mirror. Similarly, the enemies are coated in three different materials: diffuse, glass and mirror. When you blast an enemy coated in the material that corresponds to your rayguns mode, you can destroy it. However, if the material and raygun mode do not match, the blast from the raygun will be reflected according the brdf of the enemies material. To win the game simply defeat the all enemies and collected the green suitcase in 60 seconds

filmpoint average
Film Point Average is a site that aims to legitimize online audience reaction to new films by allowing one vote per connection to the site for a specific film. Films are given a grade on a 4.0 scale {i.e. A, A+ = 4.0, A- = 3.7, B+ = 3.3 etc.} based on user input. A films grade is then incorporated into the calculations for its respective cast/crew members Film Point Averages. These averages are then used to predict potential box office success.

Built using
JAVASCRIPT JQUERY HAML
