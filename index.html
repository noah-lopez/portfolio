Raytracing FPS

This FPS game utilizing principles raytracing for its raygun blast mechanics. In the game your raygun has three different modes: diffuse, glass and mirror. Similarly, the enemies are coated in three different materials: diffuse, glass and mirror. When you blast an enemy coated in the material that is the same as you current rayguns mode you can destroy it. However, if the material and raygun mode do not match, the blast from the raygun will be reflected according the materials brdf. To win the game simply defeat the all enemies and collected the green suitcase in 60 seconds
--BUILT WITH--
JAVASCRIPT, HTML, CSS AND THREE.JS

Film Point Average

Film Point Average is a site that aims to legitimize online audience reaction to new films by only allowing one vote per connection to the site for a specific film. Films are given a grade on a 4.0 scale {i.e. A, A+ = 4.0, A- = 3.7, B+ = 3.3 etc.} based on user input. A films grade is then incorporated into the calculations for its respective cast/crew members Film Point Averages. In the future, an upcoming films cast/crew members Film Point Averages will be used to predict its audience reaction.
--BUILT WITH--
JAVASCRIPT, JQUERY, HAML, SASS AND RUBY ON RAILS
